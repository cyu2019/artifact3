About Larry Woiwode

Larry Woiwode is the Poet Laureate of North Dakota and a critically acclaimed author of novels, short stories, poetry, and critical essays. He is known for a very grounded and realistic style, with the content and settings of his works tied to his real life experiences and past.

In this way, this exhibit aims to contextualize Woiwode's realistic style by viewing his poems through the eyes of a computer, allowing you to view Woiwode's work through something of an objective lens.

The poems shown are excerpts from Even Tide (1977), Woiwode's book of poems.
